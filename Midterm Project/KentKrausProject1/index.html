Conversational User Interface (CUI)

Messaging apps are everywhere. Two of the greatest benefits are convenience and adaptability. With nothing to download, no need to login, and no user interface to learn, users can tell me what they want in their own words.

What kind of things can we talk about?

Medications

Never forget to take your medications again. Ask me to create a medication reminder. Worried about side effects? I can research and share information with you. Find out more about medications like lisinopril, prednisone, or amoxicillin.

Symptoms

Not feeling well? I can help to identify possible causes and treatments, as well as encourage you to talk to your healthcare provider. You can ask me about complaints like fever, headache, or swelling

Lab Tests and Results

I can help you better understand your lab tests. Ask me about them. Try asking me about a lipid panel, CBC, or A1c.
